Sam Bordiss: Content & User Experience Designer

A Little Bit About Me

For over 20 years I have made it my mission to study every aspect of the ever-evolving web industry. This journey has seen me take on the roles of system administrator, front & back-end developer, project manager, user researcher, user experience designer, graphic designer, web designer, content designer, branding designer, and pretty much every other role with the suffix “designer”.

My current focus is promoting the importance of content, accessibility, & user experience design — three aspects often relegated to afterthoughts at best, rather than integral foundations of any successful digital project.
